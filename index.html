Showcases in Mathematical Physics is a website focused on promoting Mathematical physics by providing an extensive collection of notes and blog posts.

The website was conceived in 2026 by Alexandre Mendonça and João Macedo. Driven by the desire to creating an active online Community of Mathematical Physics researchers, where mathematical physicists from all over the world could share short notes about their research topics, using the site as a showcase for their work.
